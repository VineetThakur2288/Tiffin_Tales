Who We Are

Tiffin Tales is more than just a food delivery service — it’s a passion project created by five Computer Science and Data Analytics students from Indian Institute of Technology (IIT), Patna. As students living away from home, we understood the struggle of finding fresh, homemade, and affordable meals every day. That’s why we built Tiffin Tales — a platform that brings the warmth and comfort of ghar jaisa khana straight to your doorstep. We’ve combined our technical skills with our love for good food to solve a real problem for students, working professionals, and anyone missing home-cooked meals. From designing the website to managing databases and ensuring smooth operations, every part of Tiffin Tales has been built by us with dedication and care.

Join us on this journey and let us serve you food that feels like home.
