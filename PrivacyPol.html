Privacy Policy

At Tiffin Tales, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our website and services.

1. Information We Collect

a. Personal Information We may collect the following information when you register or place an order:
Name, Contact Number, Email Address, Delivery Address, Payment Information (processed securely via payment gateways)
b. Non-Personal Information
Browser type, Device information, IP address, Cookies and usage data for improving website experience

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
Process and deliver your orders
Contact you regarding your orders or services
Provide customer support
Send order confirmations, promotional offers, or important updates (you can opt out anytime)
Improve our website and services

3. Data Security

We implement strict security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. However, no online system is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Delivery Policy

Delivery timings will be mentioned at the time of placing the order. While we strive for punctual delivery, delays may occur due to traffic, weather, or unforeseen circumstances. Delivery areas are limited and may change without notice.
